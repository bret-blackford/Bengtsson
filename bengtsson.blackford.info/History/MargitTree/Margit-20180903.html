Margit Vanja (Bengtsson) Steele

Swedish History

  • Born: October 28, 1906 Sweden
  • Died: April, 8, 2004 (97) in Knoxville, TN

  • Immigrated: May 18, 1907

  • Mother: Agnes (Olsson) Bengtsson 1882-1954
  • Father: Carl Bengtsson 1880-1948

  • Husband: George Weldon Steele 1907-1965 (58)

Margit Vanja Bengtsson was the oldest child of Agnes and Carl Bengtsson. They lived in Stormon Sweden until immigrating to the United States in 1907. While in America the family grew.
